About me

Right now, I am at the National Institute of Informatics in Tokyo, Japan, working on my Master's Thesis in the field of Deep Learning for Computer Vision and focusing on light-weight, multi-task architectures for real-time, embeddable applications, such as Dynamic Map Creation from UAV imagery.
